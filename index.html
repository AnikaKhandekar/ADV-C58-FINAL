My Favourite Hobby
Playing Badminton

Badminton is a game that can be played by two or four players. It is my favourite sport of all time since it has become a part of me. I play to feel fit and also have fun. I find great pleasure in playing it with my friends and family. Playing a game such as a badminton is a great way of spending time with my family members. It is a beautifully crafted sport. The simplicity is the elegance in the game.
